
################################
        Data introduction
################################


chemical-chemical.csv is the relationship data between chemical compounds and chemical compounds.


chemical-protein.csv is the relationship data between compounds and enzymes.


protein-protein.csv is the relationship data between enzymes and enzymes.


classData.txt is which metabolic pathway the compound and enzyme belong to, and the number of the compound and enzyme.


aVSDxiangliangdata_0.3_300.arff is the dimension and weight required by the support vector machine algorithm to achieve the optimal effect.


aVSDxiangliangdata_0.5_50.arff is the dimension and weight required by the random forest algorithm to achieve the optimal effect.
